#  **Founding Fathers Tribute**

*Alphabetical by **first name** (A–G)*

---

### **Alexander Hamilton**

*Founder of the U.S. financial system, Federalist visionary, and influential constitutional architect.*

  “Those who stand for nothing will fall for anything.”

---

### **Benjamin Franklin**

*Scientist, inventor, diplomat, and architect of American unity.*

  “Justice will not be served until those who are unaffected are as outraged as those who are.”


---

### **George Mason**

*Author of the Virginia Declaration of Rights and defender of individual liberties.*

  “All men are born equally free and independent.”

---

### **George Washington**

*Commander of the Continental Army, first President, symbol of unity and duty.*

  “The truth will ultimately prevail.”


---

### **Gouverneur Morris**

*Principal writer of the Constitution’s final text; voice behind “We the People.”*
  “This Constitution is a glorious fabric.”


---

### **James Madison**

*Father of the U.S. Constitution and architect of checks and balances.*

  “Knowledge will forever govern ignorance.”


---

### **John Adams**

*Champion of independence, diplomat, second President of the United States.*

  “Liberty, once lost, is lost forever.”


---

### **Patrick Henry**

*Voice of revolutionary courage and defender of personal liberty.*
  “Give me liberty, or give me death!”


---

### **Samuel Adams**

*Organizer of the American Revolution and voice of the common people.*

  “It does not take a majority to prevail, but rather an irate, tireless minority.”


---

### **Thomas Jefferson**

*Principal author of the Declaration of Independence and third President.*
  “The price of freedom is eternal vigilance.”


---

# ⭐ Closing Note

Though imperfect, these founders built a framework of liberty that endured centuries.
Their words now join a new global charter — one not only for a nation,
but for **all humans, all species, and all of Earth.**
